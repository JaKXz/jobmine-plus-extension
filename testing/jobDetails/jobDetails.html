POSITION SUMMARY

As a software tester, you will utilize your creativity to design and execute various types of tests such as regression, performance, capacity, GUI, integration and functional tests. You may have the opportunity to support tools used to identify issues in the field or during testing and may also perform exploratory testing and create your own test cases as needed. The successful candidate will be able to will track and ensure issues are dealt with throughout the software lifecycle, from discovery to closure. Collaboration with multiple teams may also be necessary in order to find ways for improvement of our processes.
